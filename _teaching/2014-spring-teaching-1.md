electric machines and drives, including DC machines, induction motors, permanent magnet 
synchronous motors, and switched reluctance motors.

- Prepared and delivered lectures and tutorials
- Administered and graded assignments and exams
- Prepared and delivered laboratory sessions
- Trained teaching assistants and technicians for experiments
