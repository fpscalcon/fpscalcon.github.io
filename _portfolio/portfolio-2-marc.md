## Overview
During my postdoctoral fellowship at the McMaster Automotive Resource Centre (MARC), I worked 
on three industry-sponsored projects in close collaboration with engineers from Enedym and 
Cubic Transportation Systems, working in multidisciplinary teams of up to 15 people.

![AI-Edge Device](/images/portfolio2.png)
*AI-Edge device project for AI-powered video monitoring*

## Projects & Contributions
- Implemented high-performance control techniques for high-speed SRMs, including initial 
position estimation for sensorless operation and RMS capacitor current reduction strategies
- Explored automatic code generation for TI DSPs using Simulink, transferring legacy C code 
functionality and validating results on experimental hardware
- Contributed to the development of an IoT edge device for machine vision and image recognition 
using an NVIDIA Jetson Orin platform

## Industrial Collaboration
This work involved direct interaction with industry partners, preparation of technical reports, 
and presentation of results to both academic and industrial stakeholders — demonstrating the 
ability to operate effectively at the interface of academic research and industrial application.

## Tools & Technologies
Machine Learning, MATLAB, Simulink, C/C++, TI DSPs, NVIDIA Jetson Orin, Python, TensorFlow
