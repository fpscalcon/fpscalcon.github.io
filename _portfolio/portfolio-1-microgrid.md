## Overview
As part of my postdoctoral research at the University of Calgary, I contributed to the design, 
construction, and optimization of a campus microgrid research laboratory under the supervision 
of Prof. Andrew M. Knight.

![Microgrid Laboratory](/images/portfolio1.png)
*Overview of the campus microgrid setup at the University of Calgary*

## System Components
- Solar panels and microinverters for renewable energy generation
- Chroma battery test system for energy storage validation
- Flow battery for long-duration energy storage research
- Propane generator for backup and islanded operation testing
- RTDS hardware-in-the-loop simulation environment

## Contributions
- Coordinated day-to-day laboratory operation including safety protocols and system maintenance
- Developed and implemented control strategies for different operating modes
- Integrated and validated multiple distributed energy resources
- Mentored PhD and MSc students in microgrid-related research topics
- Collaborated with the chemical engineering group on cross-disciplinary VRFB research

## Tools & Technologies
MATLAB, Simulink, RTDS, RSCAD FX
